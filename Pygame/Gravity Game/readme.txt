
Gravity Game


Instructions:

Your goal is to get your spacecraft from the blue planet to the green planet.
Watch out, the red planets' gravity will pull you around. Use this to your advantage.
If you leave the screen or touch a red planet your spacecraft will be reset.


Controls:

Left Click  - Charge up the power on the spacecraft. Release to launch.
R           - Reset the spacecraft
N           - Create a new level



The exe to play is in the folder GGV1.0.0 and it is called run.exe.

If you want, make a shortcut outside of the GGV1.0.0 folder that points to run.exe.
